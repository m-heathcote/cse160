Camera Controls:

W-A-S-D: to move
[space]/[L-shift]: to fly up/down
Q/E: to pan left/right
[esc]: to toggle panning with mouse
hold [tab]: to move faster

Turtle Controls:

[↕]: move forward/back
[↔]: turn left/right
[O]: toggle walking animation
[P]: poke!

Build Controls:

V: turn off build/break mode
B: build mode (left click)
N: break mode (left click)
1-6: select building block
